About Me

My Hometown

I'm from Saint George, UT. Saint George is nestled in the South Western corner of Utah, just a few miles from the borders of Arizona (Southern), and Nevada (Western). I like to refer to Saint George as "Spring-Break city" because of the many recreational activities that are available within a short proximity.

My Family

My family consists of my mom, dad, step-mom, 4 sisters, 2 brothers, 4 step-siblings, and a dog. My parents still live down in Saint George, and the majority of my siblings do as well. The only sibling that lives outside of Utah is my brother, who is living in Austin, TX. Most of us gather together on the major holidays, including both my parents, step siblings, and siblings, for a big dinner. My family is extremely supportive, and I love them a lot!
